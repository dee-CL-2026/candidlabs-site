## Executive Summary

FY2026 represents a critical growth year for Candid. After the turbulence of FY2025 (distributor transition, operational restructuring), we enter FY2026 with:
- A stabilized SKD partnership
- Improved COGS ratio (45% vs 59% in FY24)
- New revenue channels ready to activate (Holywings, Modern Trade listings, Hotel Groups)
- Operational infrastructure in place

**Key Targets:**
| Metric | FY2025 Actual | FY2026 Target | Change |
|--------|---------------|---------------|--------|
| Revenue | 5.52B | 8.3B - 11.0B | +50% to +100% |
| COGS % | 45.2% | 43-45% | Maintain/Improve |
| Gross Margin | 54.8% | 55-57% | +1-2pts |
| Production | ~16K cases | 30K cases (2x/year) | ~90% increase |
| Net Profit | 445M | 500M - 1.2B | +12% to +170% |
