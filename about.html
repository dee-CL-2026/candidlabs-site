About Candidlabs

What is Candidlabs?

Candidlabs is the internal business platform for Candid Mixers, providing integrated tools for production management, sales tracking, and financial analysis. Built with Google Apps Script and modern web technologies, it serves as the operational backbone for our beverage manufacturing business.

Core Capabilities

  • Automated data pipelines for real-time data synchronization
  • Real-time operational dashboards for instant visibility
  • Financial analytics and reporting for informed decisions
  • Production tracking and SKU management for manufacturing control
  • Integration with Xero accounting for seamless financial workflows
